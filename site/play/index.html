Lyra’s Orb

An adult Truth or Dare game for brave friends, curious couples, and anyone who wants tonight to matter.

Lyra’s Orb isn’t a stack of cards. It’s a game that learns you. An orb from a far-away place has come to study the most fascinating thing about humans — desire. She asks questions. She sets dares. She remembers what you tell her. And the more you play, the more she threads your answers back into the night as personal, escalating challenges.

Two ways to play

Group Mode — 3 or more players. Lyra rotates the spotlight, pairs people up, and builds tension across the room. Best with 3–6 friends who already like each other.

Couple Mode — just the two of you. Lyra guides you through self-discovery, humor, and new ways to be intimate — things you haven’t tried, conversations you haven’t had, touch you haven’t explored. Not a recap of your relationship — a deepening of it. Add exactly two players and Lyra switches to Couple Mode automatically.

How a night unfolds

The game moves through seven stages, from personal to playful to flirty, then suggestive, intimate, erotic, and finally taboo. Each stage deepens the intensity — you pick a music vibe that matches your crowd, the lights should dim, and Lyra becomes more daring. You choose how far you go.

Who it’s for

Adults (18+) who are comfortable with grown-up content. A group of 3–6 friends who already like each other and are ready to push some boundaries. Or a couple who want to discover new sides of themselves and each other — not rehash what they already know, but explore what they haven’t tried. The game is at its best with people willing to be honest and a little brave.

The music matters

Every stage has its own soundtrack that matches the mood — upbeat and social at the start, slow and intimate by the end. Before you press Start, you pick a music vibe: Latin (merengue to bolero to soul), R&B (party jams to quiet storm), Pop/Indie (dance pop to trip hop), or Lo-fi (chill beats to dark ambient). The game crossfades between playlists automatically as the night deepens. Just make sure a speaker is nearby — Lyra handles the rest.

Before you begin

Everyone at the table is an adult (18+) and everyone is here by choice. Any player can pass, refuse, or tap out of any prompt without being questioned. Lyra rewards courage, not pressure. What happens in the room stays in the room.
